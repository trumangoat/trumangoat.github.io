Estilismo Culinario

One project while working with this food styling firm was designing and photographing the menu for our client El Portón, one of the largest restaurant chains in México. During this project I contributed to a large team consisting of the client, graphic designers, photographers, chefs, food stylists and project managers. I gave input into how to layout our physical scenes to be photographed, the choice of props and the presentation of food. I also worked closely with the designers in order to understand the brand and look we were trying to achieve.
