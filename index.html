Chris Beswetherick

I'm a patient designer, devoted to language learning, art, and exciting, playful thoughts. Check out this historical linguistics project I presented at my university on Spanish coronal fricatives. I hope to draw, design and produce content which entertains and engages users to continue learning happily. Simplifying the content of learning a language and to display it wholly to a learner is my biggest goal as a linguist and designer.
